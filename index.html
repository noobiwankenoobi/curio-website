Curio is a local neighborhood spot serving delicious coffee and liege style waffles during the day, and for three nights a week it transforms into a Natural Wine Bar with a rotating menu of wines and savory snacks. We believe in nailing one thing instead of doing a bunch of things "just okay". etc etc.
